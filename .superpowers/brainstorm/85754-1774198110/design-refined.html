Design Principles

  • Monochrome-first — White text on near-black. No colored backgrounds.
  • Blue as punctuation — Only for progress bars, dots, thin accents. Never fills.
  • Typography-driven — Large numbers, tight letter-spacing, weight contrast.
  • Subtle depth — 1px borders (#1c1c1e), no shadows. Cards via border, not elevation.
  • Generous whitespace — Breathing room signals confidence.
